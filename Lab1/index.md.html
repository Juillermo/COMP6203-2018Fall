To approach this _negotiation problem_, to be a good negotiator, you may consider: * Orienting yourself towards a win-win approach. * Planing and having a concrete strategy. * Determining which offers you will never accept. * Creating options for mutual gain. * Taking the preferences of your opponent into account * Generating a variety of possibilities before deciding what to do. * Paying a lot of attention to the flow of negotiation. ## Introduction To approach this problem from a computer science perspective, in the field of *Automated Negotiation*, this problem is modelled in various ways.
